About Us

Turning Data Into Actionable Insights

Morif Lab is a proudly Canadian boutique data consultancy located in Toronto. Founded by industry veterans, we specialize in addressing complex business challenges through innovative, data-driven solutions.

From petabyte-scale analytics to cutting-edge AI implementations, we have successfully delivered transformative projects across the healthcare, finance, energy, and public sectors. Our lean, expert team manages the entire data lifecycle, ensuring that innovation, reliability, and ethical practices are at the forefront of every project.
